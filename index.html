Toby

Toby was brought home in September 2018. He was a surprise to me! My family went out one day and said they were at the petstore getting a bird. My family are not the type of people to like birds so I thought it was suspicious. However, I let it slide because maybe my family wanted to try out something new! They come home and call me to come down the stairs. I see a cat/dog cage and thought: "Huh, was an interesting cage to bring home a bird in and why are they so silent?".
